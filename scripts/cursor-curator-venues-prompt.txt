## Official website (blocking)
1. **`website` must load** ‚Äî run `curl -sI` or fetch the homepage; if DNS fails or returns 4xx/5xx, do not ingest until you have the **current** domain from the venue (Google Maps, Instagram bio, or printed contact).
2. **`website`, `email`, and `Sources:` URLs must share the same live domain** (e.g. `kiosk-budapest.hu`, not an old `kioskbudapest.com` alias).
3. **Never reuse a dead URL** from `coverage-venue-db.json`, old waves, or another venue‚Äôs card ‚Äî verify on the live site first.

## Image (blocking)

Venue card = **this place**, not a concert poster.

1. Scrape **venue** site (`og:image` on homepage / visit / about) ‚Äî **not** a ticket or artist page.
2. Download ‚Üí `scripts/imgbb-asset-sources/providers/<slug>.jpg`
3. Upload ‚Üí **one new** `https://i.ibb.co/...` per provider
4. Audit `GET /api/public/providers` ‚Äî your URL must not match any **other** provider, meetup, or event image.
5. **Never** reuse: `cde3b78d5c56`, `cb56a463140e`, `cf91ad578e08`, `5e673e7e0093`, `038fd3264859` (known bad bulk hashes).

Examples fixed in production: Frici Papa, Vegazzi, MOM Kultur√°lis K√∂zpont each needed their **own** restaurant/institution photo ‚Äî not shared Johannes Oerding concert art.

Full rules: `src/lib/curator/imageIngestRules.ts`.
